### Note — #160 is a real fix, not cosmetic

`flake.lock` has two nixpkgs nodes: `nixpkgs` (rev `4df1b885`, a
*transitive* dep of the `jylhis-emacs` input) and `nixpkgs_2`
(rev `ffa10e26`, the **root** flake's actual nixpkgs). The standalone
`emacs.nix`/`emacs-jylhis.nix` were reading the wrong node
(`lock.nodes.nixpkgs`). #160 switches to `lock.nodes.root.inputs.nixpkgs`
→ `nixpkgs_2` → `ffa10e26`, realigning the standalone build with the
flake and restoring the cache-parity invariant. Verified post-merge that
`emacs.nix` now resolves nixpkgs to `ffa10e26`. (The verification snippet
in `CLAUDE.md` still references the old `lock.nodes.nixpkgs.locked`;
updating it is an optional follow-up — #160 only patched the copy inside
`emacs.nix`.)
